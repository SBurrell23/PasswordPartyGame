How to play
  1. 4 players split into Red and Blue teams — each with a clue giver and a guesser.
  2. The word is shown to both givers. One giver (starting with the team that last scored) chooses Play or Pass. Either giver may vote Skip — if both skip, you get a new word.
  3. On each turn a giver gives a one-word verbal clue and the guesser answers out loud. The giver taps Correct or Wrong before the timer runs out.
  4. Scoring: full points (host-set, default 7) if right on the first try, minus 1 for every wrong guess or timed-out turn your team had. Each team gets a set number of turns per round (default 4).
  5. Play through all the rounds (default 14), swapping clue giver and guesser at halftime. Most points wins!

All guessing happens by voice — the app just tracks the game.
